More About Our Work

Millier is the work of a passionate, self-taught potter who found her voice through clay. Driven by a love for craftsmanship and simplicity, she creates unique, handmade ceramics that blend function with artistry. Every piece is shaped with care, reflecting the beauty of imperfections and the warmth of human touch. Inspired by nature and everyday moments, her work brings character and authenticity to any space.
